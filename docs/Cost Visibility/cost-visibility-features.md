# Key features of Holori Cost Visibility solution

We have developpd our cost visibility solution to help you gain insights into your cloud cost and help you track and optimize them. 
Below are some key features of the tool:

## Get insights into your cloud costs from a multi account to a resource level

Holori seamlessly connects with your cloud accounts using read-only permissions. It consolidates all your cloud expenses into a single, interactive cost dashboard. You can now effortlessly navigate through the dashboard to gain insights into your cloud spending.
It becomes much easier to spot your most expensive services, individual resources, or cloud regions. 


## Create your own cost reports using filters and virtual tags

You can personalize Holori to optimize your cloud costs analysis.

With Holori’s intelligent filters, you can swiftly search through your cloud expenses and pinpoint specific resources, providers, accounts, tags, and more. Quickly identify costly resources and waste.

Virtual tagging ensures consistent tagging practices across all cloud providers and accounts. Tag resources uniformly and allocate spending by team, environment, or region without the need to re-label original resources. Achieve comprehensive coverage and improve cost allocation accuracy across your entire infrastructure. FinOps teams can efficiently create tags directly within Holori without requiring DevOps time.


## Get cost saving recommendations

Get cost saving recommendations directly in the App.
The recommendations is detailed and comes with an estimation of the savings. Once ready to accept the recommendation we directly send you to the right place to validate it on your provider's console.
