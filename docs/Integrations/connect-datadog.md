# Connect your Datadog account


To retrieve your cost data, Holori needs to be granted an access to your Datadog cloud account. The following procedure will guide you through the required steps.

In Holori App, click on your username at the bottom left of the page, then select the ""Integrations" tab and click on "+Connect now" under the Datadog logo.


## Step 1: Generate an API key

- On the Datadog console, click on your username at the bottom-left corner, under Organization Settings, select API keys.
- Click on New Key and give it a name, then click on Create Key.
- Copy the generated API key and paste it on Holori App.


 ## Step 2: Generate an Application key

- Back on your console, click again on your username, the under Personal Settings select My Application Keys. Note: Make sure that you are under Personal Settings and not under Organization Settings.
- Click on New Key and give it a name, then click on Create Key.
- Copy the generated key and paste it on Holori App.


## Step 3: Edit Scope

- Back on the console, click on the Edit button from the Scope section.
- Select the usage_read and timeseries_query and click Save at the bottom.

You can now go back Holori and click on "Save and verify" at the bottom of the page.
