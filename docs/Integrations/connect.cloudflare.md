# Connect your Cloudflare account

To retrieve your cost data, Holori needs to be granted an access to your Cloudflare cloud account. The following procedure will guide you through the required steps.

In Holori App, click on your username at the bottom left of the page, then select the ""Integrations" tab and click on "+Connect now" under the Cloudflare logo.

## Generate an API Token
- Go to your [Cloudflare console](https://dash.cloudflare.com/profile/api-tokens/)
- Go to **Manage Account** > **API Tokens**.
- Select **Create Token**.
- Select the “**Read billing info**” template from the list and if asked, for Account resources, select “**Include all accounts**”
- Select **Continue to summary**, then select **Create Token** to generate the token's secret.
- **Copy** the secret and **paste** in the corresponding field in Holori App.


Here is the link to the [Cloudflare documentation](https://developers.cloudflare.com/fundamentals/api/get-started/create-token/)

Click on "Save and verify" at the bottom of the page.
