# What is Holori? 

Holori is an infrastructure visibility and cost optimization platform that works with major cloud providers such as AWS, Azure, GCP, Oracle… With Holori, you can seamlessly navigate between diagrams and intuitive dashboards, gaining a complete view of your costs and underlying infrastructure.
It takes only 5 min to get started !

- Centralize your cloud cost from multiple providers
- Build comprehensive costs reports
- Optimize your cost with recommendations
- Use virtual tags to track and allocate your costs regardless of the providers
- Understand your cloud environment with powerful infrastructure diagrams
- Monitor infrastructure changes
- Automate infrastructure documentation
- Design and estimate the cost of your future infrastructure

It is built for DevOps, Architects, Project Managers and anyone working with cloud resources. It enables faster cloud infrastructure management and costs understanding in general.
